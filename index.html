The visualization on state-wise suicides in India provides a comprehensive overview of the distribution and trends of suicides across different regions. Through interactive charts, maps, and filters, users can explore the data based on gender, age group, causes, and more. The choropleth map offers a visual representation of suicide rates in each state, while the bar chart highlights the top 10 states with the highest number of suicides. Additionally, stacked bar charts provide insights into gender and age group breakdowns, while a donut chart illustrates the primary causes of suicides. This visualization aims to inform policymakers, researchers, and support organizations about the state-wise patterns and variations in suicides, aiding efforts in addressing this critical issue in India..

Comparing Male and Female State-wise Suicides in India
Using Tableau

The visualization delves into the state-wise distribution of suicides in India, specifically focusing on the comparison between male and female suicides. Through interactive charts and maps, users can examine the relative proportions of male and female suicides across different states. The data highlights the varying trends and patterns in suicide rates among genders, providing valuable insights into the social, economic, and cultural factors that may contribute to these differences. This visualization aims to shed light on the gender dynamics associated with suicides, assisting policymakers and support organizations in formulating targeted interventions and strategies to address the specific challenges faced by both males and females in different states of India.

Age-wise Suicide Visualization:
Using tableau

The visualization presents an in-depth analysis of the state-wise distribution of suicides across different age groups in India. Through interactive charts and maps, users can explore the data on suicide rates among different age categories, ranging from young children to elderly individuals. The data highlights the significant differences in suicide rates across different age groups, providing insights into the potential factors that may contribute to these variations. The stacked bar chart allows users to view the gender breakdowns within each age group, offering a nuanced understanding of the different experiences and challenges faced by males and females at different life stages. This visualization aims to provide policymakers, researchers, and support organizations with a better understanding of the age-specific trends and patterns in suicides, facilitating targeted interventions and strategies to prevent suicide deaths among vulnerable age groups in India.

By python

The visualization showcases a comparative analysis of male and female suicides in India over the years. Through an interactive line graph, users can explore the trends and patterns in suicide rates among males and females, offering insights into the gender-specific dynamics of this critical issue.
